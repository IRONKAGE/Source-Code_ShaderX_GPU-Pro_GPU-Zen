Demo title: 		Bloom

Demonstrates: 		How to implement a Bloom effect

Short Description:	This training course demonstrates a simple implementation of a Bloom effect

Full Description:	
This training course demonstrates a sample implementation of a Bloom post processing effect. Therefore the bright parts of the picture are extracted in the post processing step, blurred and added over the final image to create a glow around the object borders.
