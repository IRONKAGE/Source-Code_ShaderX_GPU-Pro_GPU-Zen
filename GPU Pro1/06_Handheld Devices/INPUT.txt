Input Commands:

Windows PC:

Mouse control can be used to rotate the arm in the spherical coordinate system.

It's possible to apply the rotation to the object axis, or the object itself. To switch between the two modes, press SPACE.

To quit, press ESC.

Zoom 1:

Touchscreen control can be used for the same purpose of the mouse in the Windows PC version.

To switch between the two modes, press the central keypad.

To quit, hit the red button on the bottom-right corner of the device.

Zoom 2:

Touchscreen control can be used for the same purpose of the mouse in the Windows PC version.

To switch between the two modes, press ENTER.

To quit, press U.
